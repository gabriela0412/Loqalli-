Traditional dance lessons

Learn how to dance traditional music.

description

In this experience, you will learn traditional steps and rhythms that reflect the country's history and cultural identity. Each dance represents a region or celebration, like El Carbonero or Las Cortadoras. The colorful costumes and music help bring these stories to life.

Through dance, you explore the traditions of El Salvador in a lively and expressive way. It’s a chance to learn, feel, and celebrate the culture with pride.
