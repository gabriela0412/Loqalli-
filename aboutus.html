Our history

At Loqalli, we are passionate about connecting travelers with authentic experiences in El Salvador. Our mission is to showcase the rich culture, the stunning nature, and the warm hospitality of our country while supporting local communities and promoting sustainable tourism.

Our mision

We believe that traveling is more than just visiting places; it’s about immersing oneself in new cultures, creating meaningful connections, and leaving a positive impact. We strive to offer unique and memorable experiences that allow travelers to discover the true essence of El Salvador.

Our team

Our team is made up of Salvadorans passionate about sharing the beauty and diversity of our country. We work closely with local guides, artisans, and service providers to ensure that each experience is authentic, responsible, and of high quality.

Our Values

Authentic

Ofrecemos experiencias genuinas que reflejan la cultura y las tradiciones locales.

Comunity

We support local communities and promote economic development.

sustainability

We minimize our environmental impact and promote responsible tourism practices.
