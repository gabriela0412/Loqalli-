Pupusas Making Class

Aprende a crear tu propia pupusa de tsu inredientes favoritos

Description of the experience

In this experience, you will learn step by step how to prepare pupusas, the national dish of El Salvador, in a warm and authentic environment. Our local host will share with you the traditional techniques for making the dough, filling it with ingredients such as cheese, beans, or chicharrón, and cooking them as has been done for generations.

While we cook, you will learn the history of this delicious dish and its cultural importance in Salvadoran life. In the end, you will enjoy your freshly made pupusas, accompanied by curtido and a homemade sauce, as well as a typical drink. A delicious, cultural experience made by hand!
