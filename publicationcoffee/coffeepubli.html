Learn on a coffee farm

Learn about coffee and how to harvest it.

Description of the experience

In this experience, you will learn about the history of coffee and how a family of farmers harvests it every year.

You will have the opportunity to harvest it yourself and take home a nice souvenir.You will also learn about the different types of coffee and be able to taste each one of them.
