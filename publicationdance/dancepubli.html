Traditional Dance Lessons

Learn folk dance and learn more about our culture.

Description of the experience

In this experience, you will be able to learn about the national dance of El Salvador, where you will not only practice it but also take home a nice memory.

You will also be able to see the different types of dance and learn about the history of folklore and learn more about our culture.
